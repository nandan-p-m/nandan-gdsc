Welcome to GDSC MVJCE

Here you can learn about GDSC and what we do, and also find out more about our specific chapter at MVJCE. Whether you're a student, faculty member, or simply interested in our work, we invite you to explore our site and get in touch with us.

What is GDSC?

Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) is a global program designed to provide students with the opportunity to learn and practice their technical skills, while also building communities and connections. At GDSC, we organize various events and activities such as hackathons, study jams, webinars, and more, that are focused on different Google technologies and services. Through our club, students can gain valuable experience, meet like-minded people, and have fun while working on projects that matter.

About GDSC MVJCE

GDSC MVJCE is the chapter of Google Developer Student Clubs at MVJ College of Engineering. We are a team of passionate and creative students who are dedicated to sharing our knowledge and skills with others. Our chapter is focused on developing solutions to real-world problems, and we encourage our members to explore their interests and work on projects that align with their goals. Through our events and initiatives, we aim to create a welcoming and inclusive community where everyone feels empowered to learn and grow.
